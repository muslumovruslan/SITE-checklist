SITE Talk Event Checklist

Date:
0% completed

Before the Event

About announcement email:

Who communicates with the speaker?
Is the announcement email sent from ADA SITE Talks email on time?
Is the email prepared using the usual template?
Is the speaker’s name included, and is it correct?
Is the title of the talk written in the email?
Is the speaker’s bio added?
Is the abstract of the talk included?
Is the calendar event created?
Is the registration link for external visitors provided?
Is the Teams link added?
Is the speaker and the responsible committee member added to BCC?
Is the email sent to ALL (not only SITE)?

The event venue:

Is the event room registered via rooms.ada?
Is the technical support form filled in before the event?
Is the email sent to IT about the event?
Does the speaker have special requirements for the room?
Will we be able to provide them?
Is it decided who will be responsible for checking the room before the talk?
Is all equipment working properly (computer, pointer, marker, smart board, etc.)?

Who will meet the speaker?

Is the welcome center informed about the speaker’s visit?
Is the arrival method confirmed (by car or otherwise)?
If arriving by car, is the plate number shared with the welcome center?
Is a committee member assigned to meet the speaker at the welcome center?
Who introduces the speaker to the audience?

Technical and communication tasks:

Is the technical support form sent?
Is the email sent to media.ada.edu.az to inform them?
Does the media email include event details (event description, time, location, expected attendance)?
Is the assistant dean CC’d in this and all related emails?

During the Event

Who brings the camera (smartphone), tripod, microphone, and computer?
Is the microphone arranged?
Is the video recording arranged?
Is the event being streamed?
If streaming, is everything arranged properly?

After the Event

Do faculty members meet guest speakers after the event?
Who is responsible for arranging this meeting?
Who will upload the recordings to social media?
Are recorded videos uploaded?
Are photos uploaded to social media?
